!!! abstract "For readers"

    Use this page as a **map of the wiki**. Each theme links to a **cluster page** that summarizes how the van Duin–group corpus covers that area and points to **representative paper notes** (`wiki/papers/`). Use the site **search** (press `/`) to jump to any slug. Machine-readable manifests live in the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/asepehri93/vanDuinWiki/tree/main/raw).

## What these pages are

Theme pages are **curated cross-paper views**: they do not replace individual paper entries; they group papers by **application domain** and **simulation methodology** so you can explore **clusters** before drilling into PDF-level detail.
